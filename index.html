About me

Hi, I’m Gimbler Perdomo — a data specialist passionate about solving real-world challenges through analytics, automation, and smart design.

With a background in Chemical Engineering and currently pursuing a Master’s in Computational Biology, I’ve focused my career on turning complex data into actionable insights that drive smarter decisions and measurable results. Over the past few years, I’ve worked across the chemical manufacturing, entertainment, and pharmaceutical industries, leading projects that range from automating workflows with PowerApps and Python, to building interactive dashboards in Power BI for pricing strategy and operational decision-making. Whether it's through streamlining business processes, enhancing data visibility, or integrating low-code solutions, I enjoy creating tools that people actually use and rely on.

What drives me is aligning technical solutions with real business needs—making data work for people, not the other way around.

If you’re working on something where data can make a difference, I’d love to connect!
